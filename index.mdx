## What is HuellAR?

HuellAR is a mobile app currently in development for iOS and Android. Using your phone's camera and augmented reality, it lets you see your pets exactly as they are — at their real size — in any space you choose. Interact with them, photograph impossible moments together, and save those memories to your personal gallery.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the app">
    Launch HuellAR and point your camera at the space where you want your pet to appear.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your pet">
    Select which pet you want to place from your in-app collection.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enjoy and capture">
    Interact with your pet, take AR photos or videos, and save them to your memory gallery.
  </Step>
</Steps>
