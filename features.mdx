## Feature Highlights

### Life-Size Scale

The life-size AR placement feature is what sets HuellAR apart from simple pet filters or sticker apps. Using the plane-detection capabilities of your phone's AR framework, HuellAR calculates the correct proportions for your pet's model and places it at true scale in the scene. Walk around your pet, crouch down to meet its gaze, or step back to appreciate the full effect — the perspective remains accurate from every angle.

### Capture Impossible Moments

The AR camera in HuellAR is designed for spontaneity. Once your pet is placed in the scene, a single tap captures the moment as a photo; a long press starts a video recording. You can reposition your pet, change the environment, and shoot as many times as you like — there is no limit to the memories you can make.

### A Gallery Built for Pet Lovers

The memory gallery is more than a camera roll. HuellAR organises your captures so you can find the right moment quickly and revisit them whenever you want. Because the gallery is stored locally on your device, your memories are always available — no cloud subscription required.

### Cross-Platform from Day One

HuellAR launches simultaneously on iOS and Android. The team is committed to delivering a consistent, high-quality experience on both platforms, with the same features, the same pet collection, and the same performance expectations.
