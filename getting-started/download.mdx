HuellAR is currently in development and will be available for iOS and Android devices. Before the app launches on the App Store and Google Play, you can secure your spot as an early-access user by joining the waitlist at [huellar.app](https://huellar.app). You'll receive a single email notification the moment the app is ready — no spam, no newsletters, just one message when it's time to download.

## Join the Waitlist

Signing up takes less than a minute. Follow these steps to make sure you're first in line when HuellAR launches:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit huellar.app">
    Open your browser and go to [huellar.app](https://huellar.app). The landing page is available in both desktop and mobile browsers.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email address">
    Type your email address into the waitlist field on the page. Use the address you check most often — that's where your launch notification will be sent.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit the form">
    Tap or click the submit button to lock in your spot. That's it! You're on the list.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  **No spam — ever.** HuellAR will send you exactly one email: the notification that the app is available for download. Your email address will never be used for anything else.
</Note>
