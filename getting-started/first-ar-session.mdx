Your first HuellAR session is where the magic happens. This guide walks you through every step — from opening the app and granting camera access, to selecting a pet from your collection, enjoying the life-size AR experience, and saving your favorite moments to your gallery. Follow along and you'll have your pet appearing in your living room, backyard, or anywhere else in just minutes.

## Prerequisites

Before you start your first AR session, make sure you have the following in place:

- **HuellAR installed** on your iOS or Android device. See [Download HuellAR](/getting-started/download) for information on joining the waitlist and getting the app when it launches.
- **At least one pet added** to your in-app collection. If you haven't done this yet, head over to [Building Your Pet Collection](/getting-started/pet-collection) first, then come back here.

## Running Your First Session

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open HuellAR">
    Tap the HuellAR icon on your home screen to launch the app. The first time you open it, a prompt will appear asking for permission to access your device's camera. Tap **Allow** — the camera is essential for the AR experience and the app cannot function without it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Point the camera at a space">
    Aim your phone's camera at the area where you'd like your pet to appear. For the best results, choose a flat surface such as a floor, a large table, or an open outdoor area. Hold the camera steady for a moment while HuellAR scans the environment and maps out the available space.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your pet">
    Once the space is ready, a pet selector will appear on screen. Tap it to open your collection and choose which pet you want to place. If you have multiple pets saved, scroll through the list and tap the one you'd like to see in AR.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enjoy the moment">
    Your pet will appear in the scene at life-size scale, anchored to the real-world surface you pointed at. Walk around, change your angle, and experience the feeling of your pet truly being there with you. The AR rendering is designed to blend naturally with your surroundings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Capture it">
    Ready to preserve the moment? Use the in-app capture controls to take a photo or record a video of your pet in the AR scene. The capture tools are designed to make it easy to get the perfect shot without interrupting the experience.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save to your gallery">
    After capturing, your photo or video is saved to your personal memory gallery inside HuellAR. You can revisit these moments anytime from within the app.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  For the best AR experience, use HuellAR in a well-lit room with visible floor space. Natural daylight or bright indoor lighting helps the app map surfaces more accurately, resulting in a more realistic and stable AR placement.
</Tip>
