HuellAR is a mobile app that brings your pets into your world through the power of augmented reality. Whether you want to see your dog lounging on your office couch, your cat perched on a café table, or your pet exploring a mountain trail — HuellAR makes it possible. This page introduces the app, outlines what you will find in these docs, and explains the vision behind the project.

## What is HuellAR?

HuellAR — short for *Huella* (Spanish for "footprint" or "trace") and *AR* (Augmented Reality) — lets pet owners place a true-to-life, life-size augmented reality version of their pet anywhere using their phone camera. Point, choose, and enjoy: your pet, wherever you are (*Tu mascota, donde tú estés*).

## Our Vision

The bond between pet owners and their animals is unlike any other. HuellAR was built on the belief that this relationship deserves moments of magic — moments that go beyond a standard photo or video. By harnessing augmented reality on the devices people already carry in their pockets, HuellAR creates an experience that feels both playful and deeply personal.

The mission is simple: give every pet owner a way to place their companion in any space and preserve those impossible, joyful moments forever in a personal memory gallery.
