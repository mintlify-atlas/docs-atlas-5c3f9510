The Memory Gallery is your personal archive of every AR moment you have created with HuellAR. Every time you capture a photo or record a video in the AR camera, it is automatically saved here. Think of it as a dedicated album for the impossible shots — your pets, life-size, in real places. This page explains what the gallery contains, how to browse it, and how to manage your collection over time.

## What Is the Memory Gallery?

The Memory Gallery is a personal collection built into HuellAR where all your AR photos and videos are stored. You do not need to do anything to save your content — every moment you capture is automatically added to the gallery the instant you take it.

- **All your AR content in one place** — photos and videos are stored together, organised chronologically so your most recent captures appear first.
- **Stored on your device** — your gallery lives locally on your phone, giving you fast access without needing an internet connection.
- **Automatically updated** — as soon as you capture a new photo or video in the AR camera, it appears in your gallery. No manual saving required.

## Browsing Your Gallery

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open HuellAR and navigate to the Gallery tab">
    From anywhere in the app, tap the **Gallery** tab in the navigation bar at the bottom of the screen. The Memory Gallery will open, showing all your saved AR content.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse your captured AR photos and videos">
    Scroll through your collection to see everything you have captured. Photos appear as still thumbnails and videos show a play indicator so you can tell them apart at a glance.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap any item to view it full-screen">
    Tap a photo or video to open it in full-screen view. Photos display at full resolution; videos begin playing automatically. Swipe left or right to move between items without going back to the gallery grid.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Sharing from the Gallery

Sharing an AR moment from your gallery is simple and works with any app on your device.

From the full-screen view of any photo or video, tap the **Share** button to open your device's native share sheet. From there you can send the content via:

- **Messages** — send directly to a contact as an iMessage or SMS attachment.
- **WhatsApp** — share to an individual chat or a group.
- **Email** — attach the photo or video to an email and send it to anyone.
- **Any other app on your device** — the share sheet shows every app that supports receiving photos or videos, so your options are not limited to the examples above.

You can also tap **Save to Camera Roll** to export the item to your device's native photo library. Once it is in your camera roll, it is available everywhere — in your Photos app, in other social media apps, and in any backup service you use.

## Managing Your Collection

Over time your gallery will grow as you capture more AR moments. HuellAR gives you straightforward tools to keep your collection tidy.

- **Long-press to select** — press and hold any photo or video in the gallery grid to enter selection mode. A tick mark will appear on the item.
- **Delete items you no longer want** — with one or more items selected, tap the delete option to remove them from your gallery. This frees up storage on your device.
- **Every session adds something new** — the gallery grows with every AR session, building into a record of all the places and moments you have shared with your pets.

<Note>
  Your gallery is stored on your device and is not automatically backed up to the cloud. Back it up regularly by saving your favourite moments to your camera roll, which can then be included in your device's photo backup service such as iCloud Photos or Google Photos.
</Note>
