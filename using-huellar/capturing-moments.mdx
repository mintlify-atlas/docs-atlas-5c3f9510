One of the most magical things about HuellAR is that the moments you create in AR can be saved and shared — your pet, life-size, in a place that exists in the real world. Whether it's a snapshot of your dog sitting in the park or a short video of your cat on your living room sofa, capturing these moments lets you relive them and show them to the people you care about. This page explains exactly how to take AR photos and videos and get them ready to share.

## Taking an AR Photo

Capturing a still photo in HuellAR is quick and straightforward. Get your scene set up, find the perfect angle, and tap to capture.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Place your pet in the AR scene">
    Open the AR camera, point your phone at a flat surface, and wait for HuellAR to detect the environment. Your pet will appear at life-size scale once the surface is locked. See [Using the AR Camera](/using-huellar/ar-camera) for detailed setup tips.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Frame your shot">
    Move around your pet to find the angle that looks best. You can get close for a detailed portrait, step back to show the full body, or crouch down to get a ground-level perspective. The AR model stays anchored in place as you move.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap the camera button to capture">
    When you are happy with the framing, tap the **camera button** on screen. HuellAR will capture a photo of the live AR view, including your pet and the real-world background behind it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Your photo is saved to the Memory Gallery">
    The captured photo is automatically saved to your [Memory Gallery](/using-huellar/memory-gallery) inside HuellAR, where you can view, manage, and share it at any time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Recording an AR Video

Videos bring AR moments to life in a way that photos cannot. Record your pet moving through a real space and capture the full life-size experience.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Place your pet in the AR scene">
    Set up the AR scene just as you would for a photo — find a flat surface, let HuellAR lock the environment, and confirm your pet is placed where you want it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap and hold the record button to start recording">
    Press and hold the **record button** to begin capturing video. The button will change appearance to indicate recording is in progress. Move around freely to capture the scene from different angles.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Release to stop recording">
    Release the record button when you are done. HuellAR will finish processing the video clip and prepare it for saving.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Your video is saved to the Memory Gallery">
    The recorded video is automatically saved to your [Memory Gallery](/using-huellar/memory-gallery), alongside any photos you have taken.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Sharing Your Moments

HuellAR makes it easy to share your captured AR photos and videos with the people in your life.

Once a photo or video is saved in your Memory Gallery, you can share it in two ways:

- **Share directly from the Memory Gallery** — open any item in your gallery, tap the Share button, and choose the app or contact you want to send it to. Messages, WhatsApp, email, and any other sharing app on your device are all supported.
- **Save to your device camera roll** — you can export any photo or video from HuellAR to your device's native camera roll, where it becomes available to any app on your phone.

Show your friends and family what your pet looks like standing in your kitchen, sitting on a park bench, or hanging out in someone else's living room — the reactions never get old.

<Tip>
  Experiment with different angles and distances — getting close to your pet creates stunning life-size AR shots that really show off the scale. Try crouching down to your pet's eye level for the most immersive perspective.
</Tip>
